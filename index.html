Welcome to Ironing Out Creases - Brazil!

This course is designed to work on common errors made especially by Brazilian students.

You will work on common errors and "iron out creases" to make your English as smooth and natural as possible!

Complete the lessons bellow and try to apply what you learn in your daily English conversations and writing!

Use the checkboxes to keep track of your progress!

Happy Learning!
